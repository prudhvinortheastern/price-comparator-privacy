Privacy Policy

This extension collects limited user information such as name and email address during registration to provide price comparison services.

The extension also reads product information such as title and price from supported e-commerce websites to perform price comparison.

This information is used only for the functionality of the extension and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Product metadata may be sent to a backend service for price comparison analysis.

We do not collect financial data, passwords, or sensitive personal information.
